About Zonikyo


Zonikyo is an Australian Teenager who loves playing Minecraft and stretching the limits when it comes to Computers

In the past, Zonikyo has created mutliple games using the Scratch programming website, which uses block-based code to create pretty much anything.

Posting these games to his Website, www.codedfun.net, he found a great fan base who loved his ideas. A fan base of Zero people.

So in 2022, Zonikyo created a YouTube channel "CoolD1234" and started creating Gaming content, Be like videos and other things which he enjoyed.

Zonikyo would post occasionally, throughout 2022 and 2023, but towards the Middle of 2023, there was a new thing he found fun. Livestreaming.

Ever since, Zonikyo has been livestreaming on Twitch and YouTube, creating new websites that people enjoy... and failing at school... (but we won't talk about that one ok)
